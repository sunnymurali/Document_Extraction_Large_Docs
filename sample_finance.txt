Morgan Stanley 10-K Filing
Fiscal Year Ended December 31, 2024

PART I

Item 1. Business

Morgan Stanley is a global financial services firm that, through its subsidiaries, provides investment banking, securities, wealth management, and investment management services. For a description of the business segments in which Morgan Stanley operates, see "Business Segments" herein and "Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations" in Part II, Item 7 of this report.

Financial Information about Business Segments

Morgan Stanley generates revenue from three business segments: Institutional Securities, Wealth Management, and Investment Management.

Financial Highlights:
- Total Revenue: 9.8 billion for the fiscal year ended December 31, 2024
- Net Income: 4.6 billion
- Earnings Per Share (Diluted): .73
- Return on Equity: 12.4%

Item 1A. Risk Factors

Competition: Morgan Stanley faces strong competition from financial services firms in all aspects of its business, which could negatively affect its market share in certain businesses or its results of operations.

Market Risk: Morgan Stanley's results of operations may be materially affected by market fluctuations and by economic and other factors.

PART II

Item 7. Management's Discussion and Analysis of Financial Condition and Results of Operations

Executive Summary
For the full year ended December 31, 2024, net revenues were 9.8 billion compared with 4.1 billion in 2023. Net income applicable to Morgan Stanley was 4.6 billion, or .73 per diluted common share, compared with 3.1 billion, or .85 per diluted common share, in 2023.
